    My Background

    My interest in learning about computers started in high school. I being to wonder how computers operate and how programs worked. So I decided to attend Tennessee State University to study Computer Science. Of course life events happened and I did not get my degree, but I wouldn't change a thing. Over the years, my passion for computers grew stronger and it wasn't until I moved back to Chattanooga that I decided to do something about it. So I started studying for the A+ Certification. A couple of months later I learned about the TWP 2.0 Program. I and here I am today. I am excited for all of the things that we will learn in this program and for what the future holds.
